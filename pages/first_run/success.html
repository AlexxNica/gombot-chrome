Release Notes (Dec 2012)

  • You're in a very early alpha release. As such, there are big features entirely missing. Such as...
  • There is no master password reset. It's not so bad though. You only need master password when setting up a new device or if you enter your PIN incorrectly three times. (Don't do that! If you forget both your PIN and your master password, you'll lose access to any passwords that are PIN locked.)

PIN Locking

You can choose which sites to lcok with a PIN. There's only one PIN code that you'll use to unlock your sites' passwords.

Take your passwords with you everywhere

Your passwords should be accessible wherever you are. Clients for Firefox, iPhone, and Android are coming soon.
